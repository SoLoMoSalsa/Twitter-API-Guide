Returns a single Tweet, specified by the id parameter. The Tweet’s author will also be embedded within the tweet.

See GET statuses / lookup for getting Tweets in bulk (up to 100 per call). See also Embeddable Timelines, Embeddable Tweets, and GET statuses/oembed for tools to render Tweets according to Display Requirements.
